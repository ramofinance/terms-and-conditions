# RAMO FINANCE Investment Cooperation Terms & Conditions

## Article 1 – Nature of Cooperation

- This cooperation is based on capital management in international financial markets.
- Financial markets inherently involve risk, and both profit and loss are possible.
- The Investor provides capital with full awareness of market risks.
- No guaranteed profit is provided.

## Article 2 – Trading Operations

- The Trader commits to managing trades professionally and responsibly.
- The Trader may use various trading methods including spot, margin, futures, and other financial instruments.
- Trading may be conducted through domestic or international platforms.
- The Investor will be informed in case of significant market events.
- The Investor acknowledges that the Trader does not hold an official financial asset management license.

## Article 3 – Profit and Loss Distribution

- Profits and losses shall be shared based on the agreed percentage.
- Profits are distributed monthly via BEP20 network to the Investor's designated wallet.
- In case of loss, a performance report will be provided.
- A maximum drawdown limit of 20% of total capital is defined. Trading activity will be paused if this limit is reached.

## Article 4 – Trader Obligations

- Maintain confidentiality
- Professional capital management
- Transparent reporting
- Acting in good faith toward the Investor's interest

## Article 5 – Investor Obligations

- Timely capital transfer
- No interference in trading execution
- Acceptance of market risks
- No claims in case of loss unless proven misconduct or negligence

**Important Clause:**
The Investor confirms that no guaranteed profit has been promised by the Trader and that all investment decisions are made with full understanding of market risks.

## Article 6 – Termination

- Either party may terminate the cooperation with 30 days written notice in case of breach.
- Upon termination, capital and realized profit up to the termination date shall be settled.

## Article 7 – Force Majeure

- Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform obligations due to events beyond their control (war, internet disruption, sanctions, infrastructure failure, etc.).

## Article 8 – Change of Contact Information

- Any change in contact details must be communicated to the other party.
